THE COMPANY

Ads on Top is changing the way we utilize the spaces on top of cars. We are bringing the traditional Taxi-Style advertising world to the top of ridesharing cars everywhere. Our vision is to change taxi style ads from the static single image items they are today to a constantly changing, geo tagged, location based advertising system. We want to bring the traditional taxi ad into the 21st century. Our scrappy culture keeps us motivated to do everything and anything to accomplish this vision.
