About Us

Ads on Top is a company founded by two Princeton students. David Pal is a fifth year graduate student at the university conducting research on environmental practices and graduating this year. Abhi Khanna is a senior in the computer science department. Ads on Top was formed after a hackathon when David realized that nobody was serving ads on uber cars in the area. This spurred the development of the company to what it is today.
